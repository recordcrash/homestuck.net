Help and FAQ

NSND is a list of references (remixes, arrangements, samples, etc.) in Homestuck music.

Everything from full covers to brief quotes is included â€“ even near-inaudible samples that are only known from word of composer. Additionally, if a composer explicitly says that they didn't reference a song, it's not included.

Songs that contain references have their titles bolded
Songs appearing on multiple albums have any rereleases' titles underlined.
Referenced songs on official albums are in blue.
Referenced songs on unofficial albums are in red.
Referenced songs not on Homestuck albums are in pink.
Referenced songs originating outside of Homestuck are in green.
Unofficial albums have italicized names.

For the sake of formatting, The Beginning of Something Really Excellent, Pumpkin Party in Sea Hitler's Water Apocalypse, and I'm a Member of the Midnight Crew are abbreviated by their initials.

If this is confusing, don't worry: you can just hover over any special formatting for additional info.

Referenced songs that haven't appeared on an album or were removed from the comic are listed at the bottom of the page, with links where applicable. Links to the unused Hiveswap, Bill Bolin's and SolusLunes' songs aren't included out of caution, but the Homestuck Sound Test assembled by Niklink and Sanctferum includes all of them. Any referenced songs from non-Homestuck media are listed at the end.

Cookiefonster and wheals maintain a list similar to this one for cool and new web comic.

Also check out the Homestuck Music by Artist list.
If you think that I missed a reference somewhere, feel free to contact me on the Homestuck + Hiveswap Discord!
