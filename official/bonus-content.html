Andrew Hussie's Bonus Content

A series of sketches and miscellanea drawn by Andrew Hussie during his career and released in 2025, from SBAHJ drafts to book cover concept art. This page will stay updated as more things come.

Drafts

Homestuck Aspect Symbol Drafts (ca. mid 2011)

The beta kid aspect symbols (and Terezi's, because of her Vriska shenanigans) were already known, but Hussie quickly had to come up with the other seven, and this page shows that struggle for Blood (top left), Void (top middle/middle right), Rage (left of void), Hope (bottom left and middle) and Heart (all over the damn place). As far as I can tell, Life and Doom aren't there.

Hivebent Drafts (c. Summer 2010)

Some beta designs for trolls and aspects of their SGRUB session.

Act 5 Act 2 Drafts (c. late 2010)

Some drafts of pesterlogs and [S] Act 5 Act 2.

Sweet Bro and Hella Jeff Drafts (c. 2011)

SBAHJ didn't usually have a draft stage, but whenever Hussie was travelling and got an idea, he would immediately draw it.

Topatoco Book Cover Sketches (c. 2011)

Sketches (and alt cover concepts) for the covers of the Homestuck book adaptations by Topatoco first released November 2011, not to be confused with the 2017 Viz adaptations (comparison below).

Horsaroni Sketch (c. June 2010)

HORSINOODLE, I CHOOSE YOU!!!!!!!!

Hiveswap Concept Art (c. 2012)

Early characters, worldbuilding notes, room layouts and other bits for Hiveswap.

Whistles Sketches (ca. 2006)

A sketch for the cover of Whistles Chapter 2.

Pesterquest Draft (ca. 2019)

An early draft for the prologue of the Pesterquest video game, with both shots and script.

Miscellanea

Alpha Kid Land Layers (c. 2012)

The Photoshop layers of the planets of Jane, Dirk, Roxy and the other one. Check out how the LOPAN pyramids are actually a single one copypasted over and over.

Sketchbook Doodles (c. 2014-2017)

Two Whistles doodles and two Steven Universe shitposts (combining it with Ctrl+Alt+Del and Garfield).

Beta Kids Ink Drawings (ca. October 2009)

Ink drawings made at an indeterminate date, though Jade's shirt logo was voted on by the fans so it can't be concept art. Based on the artstyle I think it's likely that it was made after 2017, or maybe even in 2025 for practice.

TSO Gazette (c. 2005)

The TSO (Team Special Olympics) Gazette is a physical zine by Andrew Hussie and some of his friends. Though the full contents are currently lost media, a lot of it has been posted in multiple forms: the forward text, some related images, and the script and textless panels for "Quincy & Carlyle's Ectobiology", a comic allegedly written by Hussie and drawn by Betelgeuse (might be the other way around?). Discord user virtuademon/roxie combined the script and panels to turn it into a finished product, which is mirrored below.
