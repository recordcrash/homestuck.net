Homestuck Browser Tools

Online tools for the Homestuck fandom, from quirk converters to classpect quizzes. If you want your website here, or if you want us to host your tool for free, use our Contact page.

Online Tools

MrCheeze

Alternian Practice

A website that allows you to improve your Alternian skills. The site will show text in alternian and you'll have to select the right English letters to progress, or viceversa.

Use
Violet CLM

Pesterlog Formatter

This website allows you to paste pesterlogs and get the HTML/AO3/BBcode codes for them (with the proper colors) automatically, as well as applying canon quirks if you want. An alternative for canon logs is ReadMSPA's search page, which will automatically copy and paste the right color codes.

Use
QuaternionMark

Quirk Generator

The most complete quirk converter on the Internet. Just type normal text and it'll be converted to an infinity to quirks at once.

Use
Miff

Other Text Converters

A few other converters that might be useful for weirder Homestuck quirks:

  • Horrorterror Text: This is just the Zalgo text tool tweaked by Mozai to be more suitable to your grimdark uses.
  • Dandytype: A converter to fancier types and unicode fonts.
  • Troll alphabet converter: Though we kind of recommend you use fonts instead, this converter quickly gives you images of Old Alternian, written backwards too. Not compatible with Hiveswap's alphabet.
Use
Ahlgreen

Homestuck Selection Screen

A custom character screen creator, Act 6-style. You will need to provide your assets, but it will output the code ready to use in MSPFA or whatever site you use for hosting your adventures.

Use
Miff

Alchemy Combinator

For those who want to try out alchemy, this lets you input two codes, select an alchemy operation and get the resulting code.

Use

Online Tests and Quizzes

RLabs

Homestuck Classpect Quiz

A 2012 title test consisting of only 12 questions, mostly based around how you contribute to the fandom. Updated on 2022 by us to reflect the newest communities and canon information.

Use
Zules

Homestuck Title Test

A 2014 title quest that gives you your class and aspect based on a very short self-administered personality test. It argues it's the cleanest, most streamline one, cutting all unnecessary variables.

Use
Zules

3.5 Part Title Test

A probably obsolete 2012 test consisting on taking three external personality tests and combining your answers to get your title.

Use
Faldanil

What Aspect Are You?

A 2013 test based around "associations" with a long list of themes. Only gives you your aspect, not your title.

Use
Stardust Rider

4chan Classpect Quiz

Derived from an anonymous spreadsheet quiz posted on 4chan, this is a really elaborate version of the test, with personality questions that you mark from 1-10 depending on how you feel about them. We find this one is eerily accurate to the comic personalities/classpect matches. (Bug us to fully mirror this)

Use
Zules

Patron Troll Calculator

This calculator uses a canon-compatible formula based on a Tumblr post by jzu to calculate your Patron Troll.

Use

Other Homestuck Quizzes

A 2014 list that assembles a ton of miscellaneous quizzes, from quotev classpect quizzes, to blood color, prospit or derse, even lands. Worth checking out if you don't mind very outdated or low quality quizzes.

Online Generators

SBURB Session Simulator

A visual session simulator, generates a group of players, or lets you design them, and puts them through the grinder. The website of FarragoFiction also comes with a sprite designer, Troll Call designer, and a few more tools.

Use
Miff

Random Land Generator

The most complete generator, assigns you a land, a title (with many fanon options), a specibus, consorts and even your chumhandle.

Use
Miff

Altblood Generator

An AU generator for the popular Altblood trend, canon trolls but with new blood colors, leading to new personality traits and dynamics.

Use
amateurTopologist

Scalemate Generator

A generator for scalemate names, which luckily include colors, so really you're generating the entire scalemate.

Use
Mozai

Nanofictionary Prompt Generator

Generates a story prompt based on six chosen Homestuck-themed cards. Based on the game by Looney Labs.

Use
amateurTopologist

Homestuck Character Generator

Another macrogenerator focused on human players. You can also select your personality traits to give you more accurate lands, specifibi and lands. Uses fanon classpects and denizens.

Use
jellycats

Fantroll Generator

Like the former character generator, but focused on a single fantroll, giving you their blood, their tastes and dislikes, etc.

Use
Armada

Chumhandle Generator

A chumhandle generator that lets you choose the initials you want to generate it with.

Use
madnessmantra

Carapace Name Generator

This tool generates a random carapace name (like Peregrine Mendicant) with the press of a button.

Use
Springhole.net

Homestuck Generators

A series of simple, focused random generators for character appearances, interests, titles, troll names, horns, outfits, symbols and more.
