Contact Homestuck.net

There are a multitude of ways to contact us:

  • Via Email: message us at homestucknet (at) protonmail dot com. The best way for sending us small files.
  • Via Twitter: message us at @homestucknet.
  • Anonymously: use our Curious Cat. Please insult us in funny ways.

There are also a variety of reasons:

  • Adding something to Homestuck.net: make sure to send us all the files we need to host the project, and the way you wish to be credited.
  • Removing something from Homestuck.net: to avoid vandalism, make sure to send us proof you made whatever project you wish to remove.
  • Fulfilling archival bounties: we'll make sure to give a small reward or favor for fulfilling one of our archival bounties below, if you wish.
