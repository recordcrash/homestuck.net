Homestuck x Requiem Art Assets

A collection of full-body art and promotional assets created for the official Homestuck x Requiem Cafe crossover events that took in place in 2023/24. These resources include dynamic illustrations, standing art, and chibi-style sprites of the kids, trolls, and cherubs.

All full-body art and most of the chibi art was created by Homestuck artist HONE. Credit for all other assets goes to the Requiem Cafe team unless otherwise stated.

Dynamic Art

Full-body dynamic poses and key visuals of the kids and trolls, used throughout the Requiem cafe spaces, merchandise and promotional material.

Standing Art

Clean, full-body sprites of the kids, trolls and cherubs, originally used for Requiem event standees and large displays.

Chibi Art

Small and expressive chibi-style art of the Homestuck cast.

Collab Assets

Assets related to the physical Homestuck x Requiem events: logos, posters, menus, decorations, signage and more.

Other Assets

Assets that didn't fit into the main categories, such as one-offs, raw layers or funny extras.
