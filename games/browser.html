Browser Games

A list of the browser games made by the Homestuck fandom, with screenshots and download links. As of 2021, Flash Games have become unplayable on browser and have been moved to Desktop games. Contact us if you notice a game missing!

TOP Team

The Overseer Project

Both versions revived and in active development by Jady Draconevix as of late 2023.
A mostly-text-only browser game, alchemy simulator, roleplaying aid, MMORPG based on the webcomic Homestuck. Grind grist, alchemize weapons, and play SBURB with your friends over the internet. Version 1 (with less features but including the Black King fight) is available here.

A daily motif identifying game, where players try to identify Homestuck song's referenced melodies for points.

Homestuck Heardle

A daily song guessing game. Listen to the intro, then find the correct Homestuck song in the list. This game currently takes songs from the official albums on weekdays, and a few popular fanmusic/unofficial albums on Saturday and Sunday.

The Prestreestuck

An Idle Game (mod of Prestige Tree) by ducdat0507 based on Homestuck. You start with layers based on SBURB Aspects, but then you gain access to a variety of layers with massive numbers.
