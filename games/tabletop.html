Subgrubs & Snazzards

An Alternian TTRPG. Play as a troll, a grey-skinned, candycorn-horned alien from the oppressive planet of Alternia that divides its people into one of 12 castes depending on the colour of their blood. 309 pages of rules for everything SBURB and Alternia. Game sessions are run on the creators' Discord server. More information here.

Years in the Future

In Years in the Future, you play a group of Exiles wandering the wastes of a post-apocalyptic world after the meteor storm caused by the beginning of a game of Sburb. It is up to you to survive the wasteland, aid the players of the game as best you can, keep a functional semblance of sanity, and do a whole lot of weird puzzle shit.
